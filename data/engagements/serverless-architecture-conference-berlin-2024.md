#### Abstract

In the ever-evolving landscape of cloud computing, serverless architecture stands out for its scalability, cost-efficiency, and ease of management. However, orchestrating complex workflows in a serverless environment can pose significant challenges. Enter Azure Durable Functions, a powerful toolset designed to streamline workflow orchestration within the Azure ecosystem. In this session, we dive deep into the world of Azure Durable Functions, focusing specifically on their application in .NET environments. From function chaining to fan-out/fan-in scenarios, async HTTP APIs to human interaction, and beyond, we explore the spectrum of application patterns that Durable Functions elegantly address. Join us as we uncover how Azure Durable Functions empower developers to build resilient, scalable, and event-driven workflows without the burden of managing infrastructure. Through practical examples, best practices, and real-world use cases, attendees will gain valuable insights into leveraging Durable Functions to orchestrate seamless, efficient workflows in their serverless applications. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just beginning your journey into serverless architecture, this session equips you with the knowledge and tools to harness the full potential of Azure Durable Functions for your .NET projects.

David Bowie might be unable to trace how time changes him, but the Azure Cosmos DB change feed allows us to trace the changes to your data within your Cosmos database. | Title                       | Going Schema-Less: How to Migrate a Relational Database to a NoSQL Database |

#### Abstract

The relational vs. NoSQL debate persists in the dynamic world of database management. While relational databases have been the go-to for structured data, NoSQL offers a schema-less, scalable alternative. This presentation delves into transitioning from relational to NoSQL, exploring the upsides and challenges. We'll explore the nuances of NoSQL, showcasing its adaptability and speed in managing varied data structures. Through real cases, we'll demonstrate NoSQL's clear advantages, such as scalability, performance, and schema flexibility. We'll also reveal the key to a smooth transition: strategically distributing data across various sources. Organizations can optimize data storage and retrieval by adopting a polyglot persistence approach. Attendees will leave with practical insights into the migration journey, from planning to execution and optimization. Whether considering migration or improving your current setup, this session gives you the tools to embrace a schema-less future. Join us as we pave the way for a more agile and scalable data environment.
