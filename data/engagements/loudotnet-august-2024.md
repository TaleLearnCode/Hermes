#### Abstract

Are you intrigued by the possibilities of creating intelligent apps with .NET and Azure OpenAI? Do you have a keen interest in leveraging the power of large language models like ChatGPT to generate natural and engaging conversations? Are you curious about how AI can build AI apps? If you answered yes to any of these questions, then this talk is tailor-made for you!

In this talk, you will gain practical insights on how to use .NET, C#, and Azure to create amazing applications with OpenAI and other generative AI technologies. You will understand the fundamental concepts of this latest wave of AI, the benefits and challenges of using .NET with it, and the best practices and tips for developing intelligent apps. Moreover, you will witness a live demo of how AI can build AI apps using .NET and OpenAI, which will equip you with valuable knowledge and skills.
