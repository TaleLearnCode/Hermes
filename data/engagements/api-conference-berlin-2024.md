#### Abstract

In today's dynamic cloud computing and software landscape, serverless computing and API-driven architectures emerge as powerful catalysts for efficiency and innovation. This session delves into their core principles, benefits, and practical applications. Exploring serverless computing, we trace its evolution from traditional models, highlighting benefits like scalability and cost-effectiveness. We dissect its components, including Functions as a Service and Backend as a Service. Shifting to API-driven architectures, we emphasize their role in modern software development, focusing on modularity and interoperability. We cover API design, API gateways, and real-world examples. Throughout, we address challenges such as latency and security, envisioning their evolution alongside emerging technologies. Join us on this enlightening journey through serverless computing and API-driven architectures, unlocking their potential for your software endeavors.
