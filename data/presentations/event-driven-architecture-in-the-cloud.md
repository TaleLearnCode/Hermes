## Abstract
Event-driven architecture is versatile in designing and integrating complex software systems with loosely coupled components. While not a new concept, event-driven architecture is seeing a new life as applications move to the cloud as it provides much more robust possibilities to solve today's and future business problems. In this session, you will learn how to create a loosely coupled architecture for your business with the domain at the core. You will learn the basics of event-driven architecture, how to transform your complex systems to become event-driven, and what benefits this architecture brings to your businesses. The overall architecture applies to any cloud stack, but this session will have examples using Azure offerings.
