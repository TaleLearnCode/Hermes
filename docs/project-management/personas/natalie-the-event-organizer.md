# Persona: The Event Organizer - Natalie

**Background:** Natalie is an event organizer responsible for planning and managing conferences, seminars, and industry events. She is tasked with finding engaging speakers to deliver valuable content to attendees and contribute to the event's success. Natalie always seeks talented speakers who can captivate audiences, share insightful perspectives, and enhance the attendee experience.

**Goals:**

1. **Find Qualified Speakers:** Natalie's primary goal is to find qualified speakers who can deliver high-quality presentations on relevant topics that resonate with the event's theme and audience interests.
2. **Evaluate Speaker Credentials:** Natalie seeks to evaluate speaker credentials, expertise, and past speaking experience to ensure they are a good fit for the event and can deliver content meeting attendees' expectations.
3. **Review Presentation Materials:** Natalie wants access to speaker presentation materials, such as topic summaries, session outlines, and sample slides, to assess the quality and relevance of the content.
4. **Facilitate Communication:** Natalie aims to facilitate communication between event organizers and speakers, providing essential event details, logistical information, and deadlines to ensure smooth planning and execution.
5. **Enhance Attendee Experience:** Natalie's ultimate goal is to enhance the attendee experience by curating a diverse lineup of speakers who can inspire, educate, and entertain attendees, leaving them with valuable insights and memorable experiences.

**Challenges:**

1. **Finding Qualified Speakers:** Natalie faces challenges in finding qualified speakers who match the event's theme, audience demographics, and content requirements, especially when working within a limited budget or niche industry.
2. **Assessing Speaker Credentials:** Natalie needs help to accurately determine speaker credentials and expertise, relying on limited information and subjective criteria to evaluate potential speakers' suitability for the event.
3. **Managing Communication:** Natalie finds it challenging to manage Communication with speakers efficiently, especially when coordinating multiple speakers for a single event and ensuring that everyone has the necessary information and resources.
4. **Reviewing Presentation Materials:** Natalie needs access to speaker presentation materials and helps to review content samples and session outlines to gauge the quality and relevance of the presentations.
5. **Enhancing Diversity:** Natalie seeks to improve diversity and inclusion in the lineup of speakers, representing various backgrounds, perspectives, and expertise, to ensure that the event appeals to a diverse audience and fosters inclusive discussions.

**How the Hermes Platform Helps Natalie:**

1. **Speaker Discovery:** Although Hermes doesn't directly manage event logistics, Natalie can use the platform to discover potential speakers by visiting their personal websites showcasing their expertise, past speaking engagements, and presentation materials.
2. **Evaluate Speaker Credentials:** Natalie can review speaker profiles and personal websites on Hermes to assess speaker credentials, expertise, and past speaking experience, gaining insights into their suitability for the event.
3. **Access to Presentation Materials:** Hermes provides access to speaker presentation materials, such as topic summaries, session outlines, and sample slides. This allows Natalie to review the quality and relevance of the content before making speaker selections.
4. **Facilitate Communication:** While Hermes doesn't facilitate direct Communication between event organizers and speakers, Natalie can use the platform to gather important speaker contact information and website links, streamlining Communication outside the platform.
5. **Enhance Attendee Experience:** By curating speakers through Hermes, Natalie can ensure that the event lineup includes diverse voices and perspectives, enhancing the attendee experience and providing valuable insights and inspiration to event attendees.

By leveraging the Hermes platform, Natalie can streamline discovering, evaluating, and selecting speakers for her events. This will ultimately enhance the quality and impact of the attendee experience and contribute to the event's overall success.
