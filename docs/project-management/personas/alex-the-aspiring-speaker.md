# Persona: The Aspiring Speaker - Alex

**Background:** Alex is a young professional passionate about a particular subject area within their industry. They have some experience presenting in front of small groups, such as team meetings or local workshops, but they aspire to expand their reach and become recognized as an expert in their field. Alex is eager to break into the speaking circuit and share their knowledge and insights with a broader audience but needs more experience and guidance in navigating the process effectively.

**Goals:**

1. **Discover Opportunities:** Alex wants to find speaking opportunities at conferences, seminars, and industry events where they can share their expertise and connect with like-minded professionals.
2. **Submit Compelling Proposals:** Alex aims to craft persuasive proposals highlighting their unique perspective and value proposition, increasing their chances of acceptance by event organizers.
3. **Build Confidence:** Alex seeks tools and resources that help them improve their presentation skills, build Confidence in front of an audience, and deliver engaging and impactful talks.
4. **Expand Network:** Alex hopes to expand their professional Network and establish relationships with event organizers, fellow speakers, and potential collaborators in their industry.
5. **Establish Personal Brand:** Alex understands the importance of building a solid personal brand as a speaker and wants to create a professional online presence that showcases their expertise and promotes their speaking engagements.

**Challenges:**

1. **Limited Experience:** Alex needs to gain experience navigating the speaking engagement process, including finding opportunities, submitting proposals, and preparing for presentations, making it difficult to know where to start.
2. **Uncertain Selection Criteria:** Alex is still determining what event organizers look for in speaker proposals and how to make their submissions stand out from the competition, leading to uncertainty and self-doubt.
3. **Confidence Issues:** Alex struggles with Confidence and self-assurance when presenting in front of larger audiences, fearing rejection or negative feedback, which hinders their ability to pursue speaking opportunities with conviction.
4. **Networking Hurdles:** Alex finds it challenging to Network and establish connections with industry peers and event organizers, especially without prior speaking experience or established relationships.
5. **Branding Dilemma:** Alex lacks the knowledge and resources to create a professional online presence that effectively showcases their expertise and promotes their speaking engagements, hindering their ability to establish a solid personal brand as a speaker.

**How the Hermes Platform Helps Alex:**

1. **Opportunity Discovery:** Hermes provides Alex with access to a database of speaking opportunities across various industries and event types, making it easier for them to find relevant events and conferences to submit proposals.
2. **Proposal Assistance:** The platform offers guidance and templates for crafting persuasive speaker proposals, helping Alex effectively highlight their expertise and value proposition to event organizers.
3. **Skills Development:** Hermes offers resources and training materials to help Alex improve their presentation skills, build Confidence, and deliver engaging talks that resonate with their audience.
4. **Networking Support:** Alex can connect with fellow speakers, industry professionals, and event organizers through the Hermes platform, facilitating networking opportunities and collaborations to expand their professional Network.
5. **Personal Branding Tools:** With Hermes, Alex can easily create and customize a professional website to showcase their expertise, highlight past speaking engagements, and promote upcoming events, enhancing their brand as a speaker and attracting more speaking opportunities.

By addressing Alex's needs and challenges as an aspiring speaker, the Hermes platform empowers them to pursue speaking opportunities confidently, expand their professional Network, and establish a solid personal brand, ultimately helping them become recognized experts in their field.
