# Persona: The Event Attendee - Jamie

**Background:** Jamie is a professional or enthusiast in a specific industry or field interested in attending conferences, seminars, or workshops to learn from experts, network with peers, and stay updated on industry trends. Jamie seeks valuable content, engaging speakers, and opportunities for professional development at events.

**Goals:**

1. **Find Relevant Content:** Jamie's primary goal is to find speakers who cover topics relevant to their interests and professional goals, offering valuable insights and actionable takeaways.
2. **Evaluate Speaker Credibility:** Jamie wants to assess speakers' credibility and expertise by reviewing their websites, including past speaking engagements, publications, and endorsements from industry peers.
3. **Discover New Speakers:** Jamie is interested in discovering new and emerging speakers who may offer fresh perspectives or unique expertise in their field, expanding their network and knowledge base.
4. **Engage with Speakers:** Jamie seeks opportunities to engage with speakers directly through Q&A sessions, networking events, or follow-up discussions to deepen their understanding of the topics and build relationships with industry leaders.
5. **Plan Event Attendance:** Jamie aims to plan their event attendance efficiently by researching speakers, sessions, and event schedules in advance, ensuring they make the most of their time and investment in attending conferences or seminars.

**Challenges:**

1. **Finding Relevant Speakers:** Jamie faces challenges finding speakers who cover topics of interest within their niche industry or field, especially when attending events with diverse programming and multiple concurrent sessions.
2. **Assessing Speaker Credibility:** Jamie needs help determining the credibility and expertise of speakers based on limited information or conflicting reviews, which makes it challenging to decide which sessions to prioritize or attend.
3. **Discovering New Speakers:** Jamie may need to find new and emerging speakers who are not well-known or widely promoted, limiting their exposure to diverse perspectives and expertise within their industry.
4. **Engaging with Speakers:** Jamie finds it challenging to engage with speakers directly, especially at more significant events with limited opportunities for interaction or networking. This inhibits their ability to ask questions, seek advice, or build relationships with industry leaders.
5. **Navigating Event Content:** Jamie may feel overwhelmed by the volume of event content, including speaker profiles, session descriptions, and event schedules. This may make planning their event attendance and effectively prioritizing sessions difficult.

2. **Evaluate Speaker Credibility:** Hermes provides Jamie with insights into speaker credibility and expertise by showcasing past speaking engagements, publications, endorsements, and testimonials on speaker personal websites, helping them make informed decisions about which sessions to attend.
3. **Discover New Speakers:** Jamie can discover new and emerging speakers through Hermes by exploring speaker profiles and personal websites, uncovering hidden gems and fresh perspectives within their industry or field.
4. **Engage with Speakers:** While Hermes doesn't facilitate direct engagement with speakers, Jamie can use the platform to research speakers, topics, and session formats in advance, preparing thoughtful questions or discussion points for Q&A sessions, networking events, or follow-up discussions.
5. **Plan Event Attendance:** Jamie can plan their event attendance efficiently by using Hermes to research speakers, sessions, and event schedules in advance, creating a personalized agenda that aligns with their interests, goals, and availability.
