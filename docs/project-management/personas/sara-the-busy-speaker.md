# Persona: The Busy Speaker - Sarah

**Background:** Sarah is a seasoned professional in her industry, recognized for her expertise, and frequently invited to speak at conferences, seminars, and workshops. She is passionate about sharing her knowledge and insights with others but finds managing her busy schedule and numerous speaking engagements challenging. Sarah is always on the go, juggling multiple projects and commitments, and she needs a solution that helps her stay organized and focused on delivering high-quality presentations.

**Goals:**

1. **Efficient Management:** Sarah wants a platform that streamlines the process of managing her speaking engagements, from submitting proposals to tracking event details and attendee feedback.
2. **Time-saving Features:** Sarah values features that help her save time and effort, such as automated reminders, templates for presentation materials, and integration with her calendar and email.
3. **Performance Improvement:** Sarah aims to continuously improve her speaking skills and increase her impact as a presenter. She seeks tools that provide actionable insights and feedback to help her refine her presentations and engage with her audience more effectively.
4. **Professional Branding:** Sarah understands the importance of maintaining a strong personal brand as a speaker. She wants a platform that helps her showcase her expertise and promote her speaking engagements through professional-looking websites and social media campaigns.

**Challenges:**

1. **Overwhelming Schedule:** Sarah needs help keeping track of her speaking engagements, deadlines, and travel arrangements amidst her busy work and personal commitments schedule.
2. **Feedback Management:** Sarah needs help collecting and analyzing attendees' feedback after her presentations, limiting her ability to assess her performance and make improvements for future engagements.
3. **Promotional Efforts:** Sarah lacks the time and resources to effectively promote her speaking engagements on social media and other channels, which leads to missed opportunities to increase visibility and engagement.
4. **Presentation Preparation:** Sarah often feels overwhelmed by preparing for her presentations, from researching content to creating slides and rehearsing her delivery, especially when she has multiple engagements lined up.

**How the Hermes Platform Helps Sarah:**

1. **Presentation Management:** Hermes enables Sarah to keep track of all her presentations in one place, including details such as topics, venues, dates, and attendee feedback, helping her stay organized and focused.
2. **Speaking Engagement Tracking:** The platform provides Sarah with a centralized hub for managing her speaking engagements, from event details and submission deadlines to acceptance rates and scheduling, allowing her to plan her schedule more effectively.
3. **Analytics Dashboard:** Hermes offers Sarah insights into the success rate of her event submissions and tracks performance metrics over time, helping her identify areas for improvement and measure the impact of her presentations.
4. **Social Media Campaigns:** Sarah can coordinate social media campaigns directly from the Hermes platform, saving her time and effort in promoting her speaking engagements and increasing visibility and engagement among her target audience.
5. **Personal Website Builder:** With Hermes, Sarah can easily customize professional-looking websites to showcase her expertise and upcoming events, enhancing her brand and attracting more speaking opportunities.

By addressing Sarah's needs and challenges as a busy speaker, the Hermes platform helps her streamline her speaking engagements, improve her performance, and enhance her professional brand. This ultimately enables her to make a more significant impact as a presenter in her industry.
